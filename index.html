About Business Analytics and Information Systems

Business analytics and informations systems is a major that is growing very fast in colleges around the country. It involves collecting data, cleaning data, and organizing it in way that is presentable. Almost every business has data in today's world but many do not know what to do with it. This is where a business analytics major comes in.
